Aurantiaco International Music Festival
Rock-Band Musical Fest
  September 15,2019
  06:00 pm
  LPU, Jalandhar
  Lovely Professional University

Aurantiaco Events are broadly focused around K-12 and Higher-Ed institutions, cherry picked to serve a repertoire of themes like Music, Sports, Academics, Dance, etc. Our Global University and School partnerships allow the freedom to keep exploring more and setting new benchmarks by introducing fresh and unexplored ideas for sustainable, reliable and successful relationships.

More than 100 Student Music Bands nominated for the Concerts held in Lovely Professional University, St Kabir Public School, Chandigarh and Pathways School, Noida to make for the most Captivating and Endearing Music Festival in the History of Indian Institutional Musical Concerts. AMF 2018 presented a platform for the finest Student Music Bands from Indian Institutions to share the stage and saw them perform with Pure Emotion. All concerts were Live Streamed Globally and saw a massive National and International audience and Music Lovers cheer for the mesmerizing performances. The Concert at The Scindia School, Fort Gwalior, scheduled for December 2019 was cancelled for multiple reasons, beyond our control. The concert would be rescheduled for the 2019 Season. The Winners from the AMF 2018 Concerts were - LPU Concert Punjab - Jagran Lakecity University (Bhopal) followed by Lovely Professional University Chandigarh Concert - Delhi Public School (Chandigarh) followed by Saupins School Noida Concert - Amity International School, Saket followed by Pathways School (Noida) and Step By Step School (Noida)
